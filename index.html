Ultra-Lightweight Virtual Human

This project presents an ultra-lightweight virtual human system designed for practical deployment on everyday devices.

Unlike many existing solutions that depend on large-scale server infrastructure and expensive multi-GPU clusters, this system is built to run efficiently on consumer hardware, including computers equipped with RTX 20-series NVIDIA GPUs, Apple Silicon devices at the M3 level and above, and even high-end tablets such as iPad Pro.

By dramatically lowering the hardware barrier, this approach offers a major cost advantage compared with systems that require several H100 or A100 GPUs for deployment. It opens the door to much broader adoption in real-world applications where efficiency, accessibility, and scalability matter.

The result is a highly practical virtual human solution with broad commercial potential, enabling advanced interactive avatar experiences at a fraction of the infrastructure cost of conventional high-end deployments.
